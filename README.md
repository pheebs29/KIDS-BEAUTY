# 👗 Kids Beauty By P — Website Guide

## What Is This?

**Kids Beauty By P** is an online shop where parents and guardians can browse and buy stylish, affordable clothing, bags, and accessories for children aged 5 to 17. Everything on the site costs between **$1 and $20**, making it budget-friendly without sacrificing style.

Think of it like a mini online boutique — just open the page in any web browser (like Chrome, Safari, or Edge), and you can start shopping right away. No app download needed, no sign-up required to browse.

---

## 🛍️ How the Shopping Works

1. **Look through the collection** — When you open the site, all the available products are displayed in a grid, like a catalogue. Each item shows a photo, name, price, and a star rating from other shoppers.

2. **Filter what you want** — At the top of the shop, there are buttons labelled **All, Clothing, Bags,** and **Accessories**. Click any of these to instantly narrow down the products to just that category.

3. **Search by name** — There's a search bar where you can type any word (like "hoodie" or "backpack") and the products will filter themselves in real time as you type.

4. **Save favourites** — Every product card has a small heart icon. Click it to save items you love to your **Wishlist** — the heart turns pink to show it's saved, and it stays saved even if you close and reopen the page.

5. **Add to Cart** — When you find something you like, click **"Add to Cart"**. A little notification pops up at the bottom of the screen to confirm it was added.

6. **View your cart** — Click the shopping cart icon in the top-right corner. A panel slides in from the right showing everything you've added, the quantity of each item, and the running total. You can increase or decrease quantities, or remove items entirely.

7. **Checkout** — Hit **"Proceed to Checkout"** to complete your order. *(Note: this is currently a demo store, so no real payment is processed — it simply confirms your total and resets the cart.)*

## ✨ What Makes the Site Look Nice

The site was designed to feel **fun, warm, and welcoming** — just like a kids' fashion brand should. Here's what makes it visually attractive:

- **Soft pinks and lavenders** — The colour palette uses gentle pinks, purple tones, and creamy whites, creating a playful but clean feel that appeals to both kids and parents.

- **Beautiful fonts** — The text uses a modern, rounded font called **Poppins**, which is easy to read and gives the site a polished, professional look.

- **Smooth animations** — Elements gently fade and slide into view as you scroll down the page, giving it that premium "app-like" quality rather than everything just appearing all at once.

- **Eye-catching product cards** — Each product is displayed on a clean white card. When you hover your mouse over a card, the image zooms in slightly and the card lifts up with a shadow — making it feel interactive and fun to browse.

- **Coloured badges** — Some products are tagged with badges like 🔴 **HOT**, 🟣 **NEW**, or 🟢 **SALE**, so you can instantly spot the best deals and latest arrivals.

- **Star ratings** — Each product shows a star rating (e.g. ★★★★☆), helping shoppers feel confident about what they're buying.

- **Hero banner** — At the very top of the shop, there's a large, full-width photo of a child in a stylish outfit with the store's tagline overlaid on it. This is the first thing visitors see and sets the tone for the whole brand.

- **"Why Shop With Us" section** — Three icon cards below the hero quickly communicate the store's three biggest promises: Affordable Prices, Safe & Quality Products, and Fast Delivery.

## 📱 Works on All Devices

The site is **fully responsive**, meaning it automatically adjusts its layout depending on what you're viewing it on:

- On a **desktop or laptop**, products show in a wide multi-column grid.
- On a **tablet**, the grid shrinks to fit the medium screen.
- On a **phone**, everything stacks neatly into a 2-column layout, and the navigation menu collapses into a hamburger (☰) button that you tap to open.

## 📁 What the Files Are (For Anyone Curious)

The website is made up of three files that work together:

| File | Plain English Explanation |
|---|---|
| **HTML file** | The skeleton — it defines all the content: the text, buttons, sections, and structure of every page |
| **CSS file** | The stylist — it controls all the colours, fonts, spacing, animations, and how everything looks |
| **JavaScript file** | The brain — it makes everything interactive: the cart, the search, the filters, the wishlist, the pop-up notifications, and the animations |

Together, these three files create a fully working, beautiful, and interactive online shop — all without needing any server, database, or internet connection to run locally.
